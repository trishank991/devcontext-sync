# Privacy Policy

**DevContext Sync**

Last updated: November 2024

## Overview

DevContext Sync ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension and related services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Explicitly Save

We only collect and store information that you explicitly choose to save through the Extension:

- **Project Contexts**: Notes about your projects, including architecture descriptions, tech stack details, and coding conventions that you manually create
- **Code Snippets**: Code blocks that you explicitly save from AI conversations
- **Conversation Excerpts**: Portions of AI conversations that you choose to save to your knowledge base
- **Tags and Organization Data**: Labels and categories you create to organize your content

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect or monitor your browsing history
- We do not track your conversations unless you explicitly save them
- We do not collect information from websites other than supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude)
- We do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary for account creation
- We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes

### Account Information

If you create an account (required for sync functionality):

- Email address
- Hashed password (we never store plaintext passwords)
- Subscription status (Free, Pro, or Team)

### Technical Information

We collect minimal technical data necessary for service operation:

- Extension version
- Browser type and version (for compatibility)
- Error logs (anonymized, for debugging)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide synchronization between your browser and IDE
- Store and retrieve your saved contexts and snippets
- Enable search functionality across your saved content
- Process payments (handled by Stripe; we do not store payment details)
- Send service-related communications (account verification, important updates)
- Improve the Extension based on anonymized usage patterns

## Data Storage and Security

### Encryption

- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
- All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with appropriate salt rounds

### Storage Location

- Data is stored on secure servers located in the United States
- We use industry-standard cloud infrastructure with SOC 2 compliance

### Access Controls

- Access to user data is strictly limited to essential personnel
- All access is logged and audited
- Employees undergo security training

## Data Sharing

### We Do NOT Share Your Data

- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not share your data for advertising purposes
- We do not provide your data to AI training datasets

### Limited Exceptions

We may share data only in these circumstances:

- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing (e.g., team features)
- **Service Providers**: With processors who help us operate (payment processing, hosting), bound by confidentiality agreements
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
- **Business Transfer**: In connection with a merger or acquisition, with continued privacy protections

## Your Rights and Controls

### Access and Export

- View all data associated with your account through the Extension settings
- Export all your data in JSON or Markdown format at any time
- Request a complete copy of your data via email

### Modification and Deletion

- Edit or delete any saved context or snippet at any time
- Delete your entire account and all associated data
- Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days

### Data Portability

- Export your data in standard formats (JSON, Markdown)
- No vendor lock-in; your data is always accessible

## Retention

- Active account data is retained as long as your account exists
- Deleted content is removed from our servers within 30 days
- Backups containing deleted data are purged within 90 days
- After account deletion, all data is permanently removed within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

DevContext Sync is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

### Supported Platforms

The Extension interacts with:

- ChatGPT (chat.openai.com) - governed by OpenAI's privacy policy
- Claude (claude.ai) - governed by Anthropic's privacy policy

We only access these platforms when you are actively using them and only to enable save functionality.

### Payment Processing

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or payment details. See Stripe's privacy policy for information on how they handle payment data.

## Browser Permissions

The Extension requests only necessary permissions:

- **activeTab**: To access content on ChatGPT and Claude when you click save
- **storage**: To store your preferences locally
- **identity**: For account authentication (optional, only if you create an account)

We do not request and do not have access to:

- Your browsing history
- Other tabs or windows
- Websites beyond supported AI platforms

## International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Extension, you consent to this transfer.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

- Posting the updated policy with a new "Last updated" date
- Sending an email notification for material changes
- Displaying a notice in the Extension

Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
