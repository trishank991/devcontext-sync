FAQ

Common questions

Does this work with all AI chat tools?

DevContext Sync currently supports ChatGPT and Claude web interfaces. We're actively working on support for Gemini, Perplexity, and other tools. The VS Code and Cursor extensions work out of the box.

Is my code and conversation data secure?

Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We don't train on your data or share it with third parties. You can export and delete all your data at any time. Self-hosted option available for enterprise.

What happens if I hit the free tier limits?

You'll get a notification before hitting limits. Your existing data remains accessible. You simply won't be able to create new projects or snippets until you upgrade or delete old ones.

Can I use this offline?

The browser extension requires internet for AI chat platforms. However, your local snippet library in VS Code/Cursor works offline. Syncing happens automatically when you reconnect.

How is this different from just using bookmarks?

Bookmarks save URLs. DevContext Sync saves the actual content, makes it searchable, organizes it by project, and syncs it to your IDE. It's a knowledge base, not a link saver.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll refund you in full, no questions asked. We're confident you'll see the value immediately.
